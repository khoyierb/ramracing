Overview

Concept & Game Design

Throughout this semester our team worked on a game called Ram Racing. Our initial idea was to develop a Mario-Kart esque game involving more of a CSU Ram theme to it. This project was challenging for various reasons, but our group believes this was overall beneficial for us to learn and understand the project management process.

Resources

Community & Forums

Unity Forums, and other sites like YouTube were invaluable throughout the project process. Especially for our group members who did not have as much prior experience in coding or website development.

Team

Team Development

Our team consisted of Computer Science and Computer Information Systems students.
At the beginning of the semester we all agreed to have a degree of interest in video games thus we came up with the idea for Ram Racing.
