Overview

Concept & Idea

Ram Racing started as a simple idea: create a high-speed racing game where players can compete on challenging tracks. The concept focuses on fun, skill-based racing with a dynamic environment.

Game Design

Our team carefully designed the track to be both challenging, while balancing speed and strategy. We mapped out car physics to provide realistic yet exciting gameplay, and designed this game to ensure it was smooth for players.

Art & Assets

We created custom 3D models and textures for the car and track. Animations were designed for smooth movement, and sound effects bring the racing experience to life.

Programming & Engine

The game was developed in Unity, and our code handles vehicle physics, collision detection, and scoring systems to ensure a fun and fair racing experience.

Testing & Iteration

Testing is an essential part of development. We ran multiple tests to ensure the game was running smoothly and met our target goal we had in mind.

Launch & Updates

After rigorous testing, Ram Racing is ready to be enjoyed by players.

Team

Development Team

Our team consists of mainly programmers and business students alike. Each member brings unique skills and experience to ensure Ram Racing is both fun and polished.

Roles & Responsibilities

Programmers handle game logic and AI, artists create models and textures, designers plan levels and mechanics, and sound engineers add effects and music to enhance immersion.

Team Highlights

We encourage collaboration, creativity, and continuous learning. Our team celebrates milestones, shares ideas, and works together to deliver a top-quality gaming experience.
