Overview

Concept & Idea

Ram Racing began with a vision to create a fast-paced, skill-based racing experience filled with challenging tracks and exciting gameplay.

Game Design

Every track is balanced between speed and precision. Car physics were fine-tuned to feel realistic while staying fun and responsive.

Art & Assets

3D models, textures, animations, and sounds were created to give Ram Racing a polished and immersive feel.

Programming & Engine

Built using Unity, our systems handle racing physics, scoring, collision, and smooth player control.

Testing & Iteration

Extensive playtesting ensured the game met quality expectations and ran smoothly on target hardware.

Launch & Updates

The project is ready for players and will continue to evolve with feedback and new ideas.

Resources

Community & Forums

GameDev.net, Unity Forums, Unreal Forums, and Reddit's r/gamedev offer tutorials, help, and collaboration opportunities.

Team

Development Team

Our group of programmers and business students worked together to bring Ram Racing to life.

Roles & Responsibilities

Coders focused on game logic while others contributed to documentation, design, and project management.

Team Highlights

We value teamwork, creativity, and continuous improvement — and achieving a working game was a major milestone!
